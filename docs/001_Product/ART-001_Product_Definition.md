# Artifact 001 – Product Definition

## Product Definition Questionnaire

### What are we building?
A configurable, cloud-native, multi-tenant Warehouse ERP Platform with integrated Supply Chain Management.

### Why are we building it?
An ERP with multiple integrated applications that work together to satisfy complete enterprise requirements.

### Target Customers
Organizations operating in manufacturing, warehousing, and logistics.

### Industry Support
Multi-industry through configuration rather than custom code.

### Deployment Model
SaaS only.

### Multi-Tenant
Yes.

### AI Strategy
To be defined in a later architecture phase. The platform will be AI-ready.

### Licensing
Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, and Annual subscription plans.

### Product Roadmap
Focus first on delivering an MVP with core warehouse and supply chain capabilities, then incrementally expand to a full enterprise platform.
