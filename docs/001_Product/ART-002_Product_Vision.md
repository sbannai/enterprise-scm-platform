# Artifact 002 – Product Vision

## Vision Statement
Build a configurable, cloud-native, multi-tenant Enterprise Warehouse ERP Platform that enables organizations across industries to manage warehouse operations, inventory, procurement, logistics, production, and supply chain processes through a unified SaaS ecosystem.

## Mission
Deliver an extensible ERP platform where business capabilities are configuration-driven, allowing organizations to enable only the applications they need while sharing a common foundation for identity, master data, workflows, reporting, integrations, and security.

## Core Principles
- SaaS-first
- Multi-tenant by design
- API-first
- Configuration over customization
- Modular architecture
- Security by design
- Cloud-native scalability
- AI-ready foundation

## Target Customers
Manufacturing, logistics, warehousing, distribution, retail, and other organizations requiring configurable supply chain operations.

## Long-term Vision
Become a comprehensive enterprise platform supporting multiple industries through configurable business modules, advanced analytics, and AI-powered operational intelligence.
